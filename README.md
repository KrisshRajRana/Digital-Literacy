# Digital-Literacy


# 📘 **Overview of Tasks**

Task 1: Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic (Module 1)

As a part of this task, I have to create a one-page infographic using Canva or Google Slides, which will describe what digital literacy is and how it is important. I have to include a minimum of three different topics in this infographic. Once I complete designing this infographic, I have to export this infographic and add a description on what tool I used and my experience with this tool. I feel this task will help me develop my creativity and visualization skills as well. This task will help me better understand concepts related to digital literacy, which are of utmost importance in today’s academic and professional scenario, along with presenting these concepts to other people as well.

Task 2: Student Digital Portfolio (Module 2)

In order to finish this task, I will have to make/edit my account on GitHub, LinkedIn, or Kaggle, which will include my details from my academic life. I will also have to add my README file on my GitHub account with basic details about myself. Screenshots of my account will also be required to be included in this repository. This task will help me to establish my online presence, which is important for my internship as well as my future life. I will also be able to showcase my interests through these accounts. These accounts will be helpful to me in the future for my growth and to establish my online presence for my future life.

Task 3: Coding & Collaboration Platforms (Module 3)

For this task, I have to open an account on a coding platform like HackerRank or CodeChef and solve a coding problem, which will be of a beginner level. Besides this, I have to create a quiz using Google Form, and there will be five questions on digital literacy. Screenshots of these two activities will have to be submitted for this task. This task will help me in developing my coding and problem-solving skills, and this task will help me in developing my knowledge on collaborating tools in a digital environment. Therefore, this task will help me in developing my digital literacy and enable me to work in a digital environment.

Task 4: Professional Email & Etiquette Guide (Module 4)

In this task, I need to write two emails in a professional way. One email is to request the extension of time to finish the assignment, and the other is to apply for the internship program. Moreover, I need to take care of the etiquette of the above emails. Additionally, I need to write the list of do’s and don’ts related to social media. I think this task will help me to improve my professional communication skills, which is one of the important aspects of my life. I will understand the importance of portraying myself in a professional way on social media, avoiding blunders.

Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness Case Study & Prevention (Module 5)

In this task, I have to produce a case study about a form of cybercrime and discuss how it is committed, who is vulnerable to it, and the consequences of being a victim of such a crime. I also have to develop a checklist of safety tips that include UPI and financial security tips and mention reporting mechanisms. This task will help me gain knowledge about online risks and how I can be safe in the online world. I will become more aware of online risks and will use the internet in a responsible manner. I will also be able to guide others about how to avoid such risks.
