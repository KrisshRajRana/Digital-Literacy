🌐 Do’s and Don’ts of Social Media

✅ Do’s

✔️ Think before posting
Once posted, it can stay online forever
✔️ Protect your privacy

Keep personal details (address, phone) safe
✔️ Be respectful

Treat others the way you want to be treated
✔️ Verify information before sharing

Avoid spreading fake news
✔️ Use strong passwords
Helps protect your accounts


❌ Don’ts

❌ Don’t share personal or sensitive information
Like passwords, bank details

❌ Don’t cyberbully or troll
It can harm others and have legal consequences

❌ Don’t click suspicious links
They may contain viruses or scams

❌ Don’t overshare
Too much personal info can be risky

❌ Don’t post inappropriate content
It can affect your reputation and future opportunities
