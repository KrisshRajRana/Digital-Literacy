📁 Part B – Stay Safe Online Checklist
Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

✔️ Verify the sender’s email address before clicking on any link
✔️ Never share OTPs, passwords, or PINs with anyone
✔️ Avoid clicking on any suspicious or unknown links
✔️ Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts
✔️ Enable 2-factor authentication (2FA) for your accounts
✔️ Update your devices and apps regularly

💰 UPI / Financial Safety Tips:
✔️ Never approve any unknown UPI payment requests
✔️ Verify UPI IDs and phone numbers before sending any money
✔️ Never scan any unknown QR codes

🚨 Reporting of Cybercrime:
✔️ Report any incidents to National Cyber Crime Portal – cybercrime.gov.in
✔️ Call Helpline – 1930 for immediate assistance
